Our Vision and Agenda: What We Stand For and Promise to Deliver

Amenities, Infrastructure & Environment

  • 🛋️

    Comfortable Visitor's Lounge

    At the main gate, with a water cooler.

  • 🚗

    Organized Visitor Parking

    Safe, designated parking inside the campus.

  • 🌳

    More Greenery

    With regular plantation drives and proper maintenance.

  • 🚰

    Reliable Freshwater Supply

    Present a clear plan for securing supply from the Municipal Corporation.

  • 💧

    24/7 Water Supply

    Activate all borewells and convert to a hybrid solar/electric system.

  • 🛣️

    Better Road Connectivity

    Engage with authorities to widen and decongest the approach road.

  • 💡

    Independent Electrical Connection

    Explore options for residential connections, enabling individual meters and subsidized rates.

  • 🔋

    Generator Backup

    For lifts & common areas, while exploring solar options and supply to residents.

  • 🏋️‍♀️

    Equipped Gym & Yoga Hall

    For a healthy and active lifestyle for all residents.

  • 🏊

    Operational Swimming Pool

    Open to all residents including tenants with a strong maintenance plan.

  • 🎲

    Indoor Games & Activities

    For children, women, and senior citizens.

  • 🛠️

    Functional STP

    Timely resolution of all operational issues to ensure hygiene and environmental compliance.

  • 🗑️

    Better Solid Waste Management

    Relocate the dumping location and explore vermicomposting.

  • 🚭

    No Waste Burning

    Zero-tolerance policy for burning garbage to maintain air quality and health.

Safety & Security

  • 🛡️

    Strengthened Security For All

    Periodic training for security staff and system upgrades (CCTV, Visitor Apps).

  • 🚧

    Enhanced Perimeter Security

    Install Y-angle brackets and barbed wire on boundary walls.

  • 📱

    App-Based Society Management

    Explore apps like MyGate for access control and complaint resolution.

  • 🚪

    Smooth & Secure Main Gate

    Efficient and safe entry for residents and visitors.

  • 🔥

    Fire & Lift Safety

    Technical reviews, regular drills, and auto fire safety balls in server rooms.

  • 🏃

    Clear Evacuation Plans

    Visible plans and drills for emergency preparedness.

  • 🤸

    Safer Children's Play Area

    Install fencing along the park path to protect children from vehicle traffic.

Transparency, Fairness & Community

  • 📢

    Transparent Communication

    Regular updates on society matters via digital notice boards & official WhatsApp groups.

  • 💻

    Online Meeting Access

    Allow members to join important meetings via MS Teams or Zoom or Google platform.

  • 📝

    Resident Participation

    Use online surveys to get views on new initiatives and form resident committees.

  • 🤫

    Respect for Privacy

    MC will not intervene in personal choices within legal/community norms.

  • 🏡

    Resident Autonomy

    Freedom to install balcony screens/grills that don't affect building structure.

  • ⚖️

    Review of By-laws

    Amend rules to ensure they are fair and provide equal rights to everyone.

  • 🤝

    End Unfair Policies

    Provide equal rights to tenants to access all amenities and services.

  • ⏰

    Flexible Interior Work Hours

    Extend timings to provide more flexibility for flat improvements.

  • 🚺

    Female Conservancy Staff

    Prioritize hiring of female staff for roles interacting with families.

  • 📋

    Clear Roles & Responsibilities

    For all society staff to improve accountability and service quality.

  • 🧾

    Financial Prudence

    Clear, audited accounting with timely budgeting and cost-saving measures.

  • 🕊️

    Peaceful & Harmonious Living

    Promote unity through cultural and social events for a proud community.
